About

The Artist

Profile photo
[replace placeholder]

elli mia is a Japanese electronic music producer, DJ, and artist based in Tsukuba, Ibaraki. Drawing from deconstructed club, darkstep, and experimental electronica, her music blurs the boundaries between the dance floor and the avant-garde — with Hatsune Miku as a frequent creative collaborator.

Her debut full-length album exonorm (2026) weaves a cosmic narrative around a civilisation caught in the gravitational pull of a binary star system, featuring dense sound design and bilingual lyrics across ten tracks. Her earlier EP animacy (2023) introduced her signature blend of Vocaloid production and intricate rhythmic textures to the underground electronic scene.

Beyond the studio, elli mia is an active DJ and event organiser — co-founding the purewire event series and performing at clubs across Japan, including OctBass, CIRCUS TOKYO, and Wall&Wall. She also takes on vocal mixing, arrangement, and commissioned production work.
